Han-nya Japanese Restaurant

Han-nya Japanese Restaurant is open 24 hours daily. It is located at the ground floor of Hotel Asia. For breakfast choices, you can have Japanese, American/Western and Filipino dishes. You can enjoy more than 100 kinds of dishes such as fried or grilled fish, nigiri sushi, buckwheat noodle, etc. at very affordable prices. You can also enjoy different dishes every day for one month!We serve not only meals but also fresh fruits, ice cream, coffee, etc.. The meaning of "Han-nya" is "Wisdom of Buddha.
